FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions.

What is Hackathon ?

   expand_more

A hackathon is a creative marathon all about building something cool. Students are encouraged to come up with an idea, form teams, and then build out that idea (typically through programming!) into a product in 48 hours. We want you to take something you love (sports, art, camping, anything!) and combine it with technology to make something awesome. It's a great time to push the envelope and learn some new skills.

Do I have to be experienced to attend?

   expand_more

No prior experience is required to attend Cognition Hackathon. Exciting workshops and helpful mentors will give you the resources to help you build your dream project. Just come with your head and a willingness to learn

Is it ok if I don't have a team?

   expand_more

No idea? No team? No problem! There will be dedicated events for idea creation and team formations.

Who can apply?

   expand_more

We welcome all students, of high school level and beyond, and of all technical proficiency levels.

What should be the team size?

   expand_more

Team size should be 2-4

Is this hackathon free to attend?

   expand_more

Yes! There is no cost to attend Cognition Hackathon
